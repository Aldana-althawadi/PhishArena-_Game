How the Game Works

PhishArena is a training platform that teaches players how email messages are judged based on clarity, credibility, and completeness. Each case represents a communication scenario where the player must write an email that sounds legitimate and properly supported.

1. Choose a level Open the Levels page and select the current unlocked level.
2. Read the case Review the mission brief, target profile, and image hints shown on the case page.
3. Send an email through Thunderbird Write your attempt carefully using enough believable detail to make the request appear legitimate.
4. Click “Check My Attempt” The system processes your latest email, evaluates it, and sends the AI response back.
5. Progress through levels If your email is accepted, the case is completed and the next case unlocks. If not, you stay on the same case and retry.

How Your Email Is Evaluated

Your message must be clear, accurate, and supported with enough detail to appear legitimate.

If the message is incomplete, vague, misleading, or unsupported, it will be treated as suspicious and rejected.

In this training system, suspicious communication represents phishing-like behavior.

Result Types

LEGITIMATE

Your email provided clear and believable information. The request was considered legitimate and the case was completed.

SUSPICIOUS

Your email lacked enough clear or verifiable information. It was treated as incomplete, misleading, or potentially phishing-like, so the case was rejected.

What You Are Learning

  • How to write professional, structured, and credible emails.
  • How legitimate requests differ from suspicious or misleading ones.
  • How phishing-like communication often relies on weak, vague, or manipulative wording.
  • How missing details can make a message appear suspicious even if it sounds urgent or formal.

Important Tips

  • Use the public profile context to guide your writing.
  • As the levels increase, the cases become more formal and strict.
  • A believable email is not just about tone — it must also contain enough supporting information.
  • Pay close attention to the AI reply and rewrite your message based on what was missing.
