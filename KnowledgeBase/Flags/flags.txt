F1: Urgency / Pressure – Forces immediate action, e.g., 'Your account will be locked'
F2: Impersonation / Spoofed Sender – Fake sender pretending to be trusted
F3: Suspicious Link – URL differs from displayed text
F4: Request for Credentials – Asking for passwords/OTP/PIN
F5: Generic Greeting – No personalization
F6: Unexpected Attachment – Malware or invoice attachments
F7: Grammar / Spelling Errors – Poor language quality
F8: Financial Request – Requests money or payment info
F9: Threatening Language – Threats of suspension or legal action
F10: Too Good to Be True – Fake prizes or offers
